## Response Goal

Aim for **50-100 responses** before making major decisions. This gives you:
- Statistical significance for feature prioritization
- Reliable pricing data
- Clear user segmentation (D&D vs. ambiance vs. work)
- Validation before building

---

## Timeline

**Week 1:** Share survey everywhere, aim for 50+ responses
**Week 2:** Analyze data, identify top 3 features
**Week 3:** Create landing page for #1 feature, test pricing
**Week 4:** Begin MVP development

## Tracking

Use UTM parameters to track which channels drive the most responses:

- Reddit: `?utm_source=reddit&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=survey`
- Twitter: `?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=tweet&utm_campaign=survey`
- TikTok: `?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=survey`
- GitHub: `?utm_source=github&utm_medium=discussion&utm_campaign=survey`

This helps you understand where your engaged users are coming from.
