## Filming Checklist

**Equipment:**
- iPhone/smartphone (4K)
- Tripod or stable surface
- Good lighting (ring light or natural)
- Projector
- Clean room background

**Shots to capture:**
1. Empty wall (wide shot)
2. Projector turning on
3. Phone showing site + casting
4. Multiple ambience themes projected
5. Cozy room shots (multiple angles)
6. Close-ups of projector
7. Before/after comparisons
