# Projector Brand Affiliate Program Research
*Research conducted: February 12, 2026*

## Executive Summary

Researched 5 major projector brands for affiliate partnership opportunities. Commission rates range from 5-18% depending on brand and region. Mix of direct programs and affiliate network platforms.

---

## 1. NEBULA (Anker/soundcore)

### Program Details
- **Commission Rate**: 5% (Referral), 8-18% AOV (Regional Affiliate)
  - Nebula DE: Up to 18% AOV
  - Nebula UK: Up to 12% AOV
  - Nebula US: Up to 12% AOV
- **Cookie Duration**: 30 days (standard Anker program)
- **Program Type**: Direct + Affiliate Networks

### Contact Information
- **Affiliate Contact**: support@seenebula.com
- **Application Page**: https://www.seenebula.com/affiliate
- **Referral Program**: https://www.seenebula.com/referral

### Program Requirements
- Review sites, online communities, bloggers, content creators
- Select affiliate applications (not auto-approval)
- Program manager approval required

### Notes
- Part of larger Anker ecosystem (8% standard commission on Anker products)
- Strong brand recognition through Anker parent company
- Active in multiple affiliate networks including Webgains
- Recent product launches (Nebula P1 in Europe, P1i for $369 in 2026)

---

## 2. XGIMI

### Program Details
- **Commission Rate**: 5% per sale
- **Cookie Duration**: 30 days
- **Payment Terms**: Commission paid 60 days after purchase (no refund requests)
- **Program Type**: Direct + ShareASale

### Contact Information
- **Affiliate Contact**: affiliate@xgimi.com
- **Business Inquiries**: business@xgimi.com
- **PR Inquiries**: media@xgimi.com
- **Application Page**: https://us.xgimi.com/pages/affiliate-guide

### Program Requirements
- Open to everyone (public application)
- Simple approval process
- Monthly payment schedule

### Notes
- Very responsive affiliate program (dedicated email contact)
- Active ShareASale presence
- Recent CES 2026 presence (TITAN Noir Max launch)
- 30-day attribution window for all customer purchases
- Also operates on Awin network (merchant ID: 77150)

---

## 3. ANKER (Nebula Parent Brand)

### Program Details
- **Commission Rate**: 8% standard, 8-15% Nordic tiered program
- **Cookie Duration**: 30 days (above industry norm)
- **Platform**: Impact Affiliate Platform
- **Program Type**: Direct via Impact

### Contact Information
- **Application Page**: https://www.anker.com/become-an-affiliate
- **Network**: Impact Platform (not ShareASale)
- **Support**: Dedicated Affiliate Manager provided

### Program Requirements
- Open application
- Includes Nebula projector products
- Access to comprehensive affiliate tools (banners, data feeds)

### Notes
- Broader product portfolio than just projectors
- Strong infrastructure and affiliate support
- Not currently on ShareASale despite some sources suggesting it
- Nordic program offers higher tiered commissions (8-15%)

---

## 4. BenQ

### Program Details
- **Commission Rate**: Not publicly disclosed
- **Program Tiers**: Platinum, Gold, Silver
- **Program Type**: Application-based (selective)
- **Benefits**: Product discounts, branded coupons, promotional items

### Contact Information
- **Business Email**: BBS@BenQ.com
- **Sales Phone**: 888-818-5888
- **Office**: 3200 Park Center Dr, Costa Mesa, CA 92626
- **Contact Form**: https://www.benq.com/en-us/business/support/contact-us/email-us.html
- **B2B Contact**: https://campaigns.benq.com/contact-us-b2b

### Program Requirements
- Application required (not guaranteed acceptance)
- 3-tier program structure
- Agreement to terms and services
- Appears more focused on B2B partnerships than standard affiliate

### Notes
- More selective than other programs
- Stronger emphasis on business partnerships and resellers
- Available on FlexOffers and VigLink networks
- Also offers sponsorship programs for content creators/influencers
- Partner portal for business solutions

## 5. EPSON

### Program Details
- **Commission Rate**: Not publicly disclosed
- **Program Type**: Epson Advantage Partner Program (6 categories, 3 tiers)
- **Tiers**: Silver, Gold, Platinum
- **Cookie Duration**: 14 days (shorter than competitors)

### Contact Information
- **Sales Representative**: 1-800-947-8247
- **Partner Program**: https://epson.com/epson-partners-program
- **Partner Portal**: https://epson.com/epson-partner-portal

### Program Requirements
- Reseller/partner focused (not traditional affiliate)
- Criteria: product focus, expertise, sales level, support offerings
- Application process required
- Appears to prioritize established businesses

### Notes
- Available on FlexOffers, VigLink, Commission Junction, Brandreward
- More B2B/reseller oriented than consumer affiliate
- Includes printers, scanners, projectors, supplies
- Financial incentives, marketing support, tech support provided
- 14-day cookie is shortest among researched brands

## Recommendations for Projector Windows

### Tier 1 - Immediate Outreach (Easiest + Best Terms)
1. **XGIMI** - Most accessible, clear 5%, dedicated affiliate contact
2. **Anker/Nebula** - Strong commission (8-12%), good support, established platform

### Tier 2 - Secondary Outreach
3. **BenQ** - Selective but worth applying, good brand recognition
4. **Epson** - B2B focused but projector line is strong

### Strategic Notes
- **Focus pitch on**: Content creation (projector reviews, comparison content)
- **Emphasize**: Traffic metrics, audience quality, content strategy
- **Projector Windows unique value**: Dedicated projector content, buying guides, comparisons
- **Start with**: XGIMI and Anker for quick wins, then leverage success for BenQ/Epson
