# Projector Brand Partnership Research

Research conducted: February 12, 2026

## Overview

This document contains research on affiliate and partnership programs for five major projector brands. The goal is to establish partnerships to promote Projector Windows as a complementary software solution.

---

## 1. Nebula (Anker subsidiary)

### Affiliate Program
- **Commission**: 3.92% (US) / up to 12% AOV (UK)
- **Cookie Duration**: 30 days
- **Payout Timeline**: Commission paid 60 days after verified purchase
- **Restrictions**:
  - No PPC allowed
  - No paid social media
  - No popup/siteunder traffic
  - No standalone email campaigns (newsletter inclusion OK)

### Referral Program (Alternative)
- **Reward**: $50 SeeNebula.com gift card per successful referral
- **Eligibility**: US residents only
- **Verification**: 30-day verification period after purchase

### Program Links
- Affiliate Program: https://www.seenebula.com/affiliate
- Referral Program: https://www.seenebula.com/referral

### Contact Strategy
- Apply through affiliate program for standard commission structure
- Consider referral program as secondary option
- Highlight software integration benefits for their smart projector lineup

### Notes
- Nebula is Anker's projector brand (soundcore Nebula)
- Recent launches: P1, P1i portable projectors with Google TV
- Strong focus on portable, smart projectors with built-in speakers

---

## 2. XGIMI

### Affiliate Program
- **Commission**: 5% per sale (US site)
- **Cookie Duration**: 30 days
- **Payout Timeline**: Commission paid 60 days after purchase (no refunds)
- **Cap**: No commission cap - unlimited earning potential

### Partnership Options
- Standard affiliate program open to everyone
- Deeper partnership opportunities available upon request through affiliate team

### Program Links
- US Affiliate Guide: https://us.xgimi.com/pages/affiliate-guide
- Global Affiliate Guide: https://global.xgimi.com/pages/affiliate-guide
- Available through Awin network: https://ui.awin.com/merchant-profile/77150

### Contact Strategy
- Start with standard affiliate application
- Request exploratory call for deeper partnership discussing:
  - Software integration opportunities
  - Co-marketing possibilities
  - Technical collaboration for Projector Windows optimization

### Notes
- Active at CES 2026 with new TITAN Noir Max launch
- Strong 4K and laser projector lineup
- Positioning as professional-grade projection made accessible

## 3. Anker (including Nebula brand)

### Affiliate Program
- **Commission**: 8% per sale (anker.com US)
- **Commission**: 7% per sale (UK)
- **Commission**: 8% per sale (EU-DE)

### SuperChargers Content Creator Program
- Separate program for content creators
- Link: https://www.anker.com/content-creator

### Program Links
- Main Affiliate Program: https://www.anker.com/become-an-affiliate
- AnkerWork Affiliate: https://us.ankerwork.com/pages/become-an-affiliate
- Available through Webgains (UK): https://www.webgains.com/front/publisher/program/view/programID/289655

### Contact Strategy
- Apply to main Anker affiliate program (higher commission than Nebula direct)
- Consider SuperChargers program for content/demo videos
- Position Projector Windows as enhancing their Nebula projector ecosystem

### 2026 Projector Lineup
- Nebula X1 Pro (released Jan 20, 2026)
- Nebula P1i ($369, early 2026)
- Nebula P1 (now in Europe)
- Focus on portable, all-in-one entertainment solutions

## 4. BenQ

### Affiliate Program
- **Structure**: 3-tier system (Platinum, Gold, Silver)
- **Cookie Duration**: 45 days
- **Application**: Acceptance not guaranteed - review process
- **Network**: Available through FlexOffers.com

### Benefits (if accepted)
- Discounts on BenQ products
- Branded coupons for commissions
- Promotional items
- Marketing support

### Program Links
- Business Partners: https://www.benq.com/en-us/business/software/solution-partner.html
- Partner Portal: https://campaigns.benq.com/educationpartners.com
- FlexOffers Program: https://www.flexoffers.com/affiliate-programs/benq-america-affiliate-program/

### Contact Strategy
- Submit formal application emphasizing:
  - Software solution (not competing hardware)
  - Enhances BenQ projector experience
  - Target audience alignment (gaming, business, education)
- Consider applying as Display Solution Partner

### Notes
- Strong in gaming sponsorships (potential angle)
- Recent BR9708 4K cinema-grade projector for attractions
- Focus on monitors, projectors, digital signage
- Education and business markets

## 5. Epson

### Epson Advantage Partner Program
- **Structure**: 3 tiers (Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- **Focus**: Six categories for different business markets
- **Evaluation**: Based on product focus, expertise, sales, support offerings

### Benefits
- Financial incentives
- Incremental product solutions
- Marketing support
- Tech support

### Program Links
- Partner Program: https://epson.com/epson-partners-program
- Partner Portal: https://epson.com/epson-partner-portal
- Consultant Program: https://epson.com/partners-consultant-program

### Contact Strategy
- This is a B2B partner program, not traditional affiliate
- Position as software solution provider enhancing Epson projector value
- Highlight AV/engineering consulting angle if applicable
- May require more formal business relationship

### 2026 Initiatives
- Lifestudio projector line with Google TV + Gemini AI integration
- Official Projector Partner of Cirque du Soleil
- Strong partnerships with enterprise/entertainment

### Notes
- Most formal partner program of the five brands
- Best suited for B2B relationship rather than affiliate marketing
- Higher barrier to entry but potentially more valuable partnership

---

## Summary Comparison

| Brand | Commission/Model | Barrier to Entry | Best Approach |
|-------|------------------|------------------|---------------|
| **Nebula** | 3.92-12% affiliate OR $50 gift card | Low | Direct affiliate application |
| **XGIMI** | 5% affiliate | Low | Affiliate + request partnership call |
| **Anker** | 8% affiliate | Low | Anker affiliate (better than Nebula direct) |
| **BenQ** | Tiered affiliate | Medium | Formal application emphasizing software synergy |
| **Epson** | B2B partner program | High | Business partnership pitch, not affiliate |

---

## Recommended Priority Order

1. **Start with: Anker/XGIMI/Nebula** - Easy affiliate sign-up, projector-focused
2. **Follow up with: BenQ** - Submit partner application after proving concept with others
3. **Long-term: Epson** - Pursue formal business partnership once established
