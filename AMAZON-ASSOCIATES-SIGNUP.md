## Signup Process

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/
2. Click "Sign up" or "Join now for free"
3. Use existing Amazon account or create new one
4. Choose account type: Individual (recommended for starting)

## Troubleshooting

**Application rejected?**
- Ensure site has enough content (10+ pages recommended)
- Add privacy policy
- Remove any placeholder content
- Reapply after 7 days

**No clicks showing?**
- Verify Associate ID is correct in links
- Check for cookie blockers
- Wait 24 hours for dashboard to update

**Account closed due to inactivity?**
- Must make 3 sales within first 180 days
- Post more content and promote actively
- Can reapply after 30 days
